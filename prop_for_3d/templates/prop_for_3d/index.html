Metal 3D printing is a growing alternative method to manufacture complex parts and for most applications is a well-established process. The method is based on selective laser or electron-beam melting of layers of metal powder.



Using additive manufacturing approach, we aim to create metal micro-sized surfaces/coatings having intricate structures directly on selected solid metal substrates.



This is a novel approach of using this technique to create functional surfaces directly on substrates aiming to reduce manufacturing and post processing cost, CO2 emission, product longevity, and especially the possibility to develop the manufacturing upscaling.
